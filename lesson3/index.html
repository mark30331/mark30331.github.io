Site Purpose

This website provides you with weather forecast. The purpose of forecasting is to prepare people and businesses from losing money and to enhance human comfort. Good forecasts pay off economically not by generating large sums of money but rather by preventing the loss of a large sum of money.where you can see extreme weather near you.
